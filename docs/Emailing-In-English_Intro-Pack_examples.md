## Series 1 — Effective Emailing (ejemplos)
| Categoría | Ejemplos |
| --- | --- |
| Subject (Maximum of 5 words) | Please respond by 7 November; Project X cost report; Budget meeting 10 October |
| Formal Greeting | Dear Sir / Madam,; Dear Ms.Davila, / Dear Marta Davila,; Dear Marta, |
| Informal Greeting | Hi all,; Hello everyone,; Hi Maria,; Maria, |
| Formal Opening (set 1) | I’m writing with reference to your email 10 October 2012.; My name is Roberto Aguila and I work for ADADE.; It was a pleasure to meet you at the SIMO trade conference. |
| Formal Opening (set 2) | It was good to speak to you by phone yesterday.; Thank you for emailing me the report.; Sorry I can’t make the meeting because I’m travelling. |
| Informal Opening | Thanks for doing that.; Great idea!; I’d like you to review the report and give me feedback. |
| Purpose for emailing — Information (set 1) | I’m writing to you to request information about your new product line.; TDC is an IT Consultancy and we are interested in your new product line.; We need to know when your new product line will be launched. |
| Purpose for emailing — Information (set 2) | Could you send me your company’s price catalogue?; Can you send me your company’s product catalogue?; I’d be grateful if you could send the product specifications.; Please provide information about the product specifications. |
| Purpose for emailing — Action (set 1) | I’d like you to send a proposal by the end of this week.; We’d like you to provide a budget proposal.; Would you mind sending me the proposal tomorrow?; Do you think you could complete the work by Tuesday? |
| Purpose for emailing — Action (set 2) | Could you finish the work by the end of this week?; Can you reach a decision by the end of the week?; It would be helpful if you could email it tomorrow. |
| Purpose for emailing — Help | Do you need me to do anything else?; Would you like any more information?; Let me know if I can do anything more to help.; That would be extremely helpful thank you.; I would like to thank you for your professionalism. |
| Purpose for emailing — Apologise (set 1) | I am writing in respect to your recent complaint.; I was very concerned to learn about your complaint dated 2 March 2012.; I would like to apologise for the incident of 3 April. |
| Purpose for emailing — Apologise (set 2) | I will look into the matter and get back to you tomorrow.; I can assure you that it will not happen again.; Please accept my apologies for any inconvenience. |
| Additional Information | Additional information is provided in the attached files.; Please see the attached file for additional information.; I’ve attached additional information. |
| Closing email | I look forward to hearing from you shortly.; We look forward to hearing from you.; If you need any further information please don’t hesitate to contact me.; If you have any further questions please contact me. |
| End | Best regards,; Regards,; Kind regards, |

## Series 2 — Opening and Closing (ejemplos)

| --- | --- |
| Subject (Maximum of 5 words) — set 1 | Agenda for the meeting 10/11/2013; Updates on the building plans; Meeting scheduled for 17/10 at 10am |

| Formal Greeting | Dear Ms.Davila,; Dear Marta Davila,; Dear Mr Yuang, |
| Opening Sentences (Follow-up) — set 1 | With respect to our phone conversation, I am sending you the monthly sales report.; Further to our last discussion, I would like to bring to your attention the following issues.; Following on from our meeting 7 January, I propose we meet again in the first week of February. |
| Opening Sentences (Follow-up) — set 2 | As we agreed let´s meet again in one month.; As we discussed in our meeting 6 April, I suggest we meet next to discuss Phase 2´s milestones.; As agreed by email on 9 April, I propose we meet at the end of April. |
| Opening Sentences (With reference to something) — set 1 | In your last email you mentioned that you were waiting for TYZ to send you the project time line.; In your email dated 07 March you asked for confirmation of the changes made.; With regards to Phase 2, I have listed below the tasks and deadlines that need to be completed. |
| Opening Sentences (With reference to something) — set 2 | I am writing with reference to your email 7 April.; I am writing in regard to our last meeting.; I am writing with respect to our last meeting. |
| Opening Sentences (Someone you have met recently) | It was a pleasure to meet you at the THO Trade Fair in Manila last week.; It was good to meet you at the THO Trade Fair.; I enjoyed meeting you at THO Trade Fair.; We met at Singapore´s World Mobile Conference at your company´s stand. |
| Opening Sentences (Attaching documents) | Attached please find the documents you requested regarding our TZT product line.; Please find attached the documents you asked for.; I´ve attached the following document, “TZT Specs”. |
| Closing Sentences — set 1 | Your assistance in getting this information as soon as possible is appreciated.; I appreciate your assistance in this matter.; Thanks for taking the time to consider this issue. |

## Series 3 — Making Enquiries and Placing Orders (ejemplos)
| Categoría | Ejemplos |
| --- | --- |
| Subject (Maximum of 5 words) | Product Enquiry AP34; Order Enquiry 09/09/2012; Purchasing Order AP34 |
| Enquiries — set 1 | We are a manufacturing company in Indonesia. We are interested in your Product Series PS-2TZ.; Could you email me the updated product information?; Please send me your latest price list. |
| Enquiries — set 2 | This email is to enquire about Product Series PS-2TZ.; This email is to request information about PS-2TZ. |
| Enquiries — set 3 | I am writing to enquire about Product Series 2TZ.; I am writing to request more information about Product Series 2TZ. |
| Placing Orders — set 1 | We would like to order 15 units of PS-2TZ.; I am writing to order 15 units of PS-2TZ.; I want to order 15 units of PS-2TZ.; We´re interested in purchasing 15 units of PS-2TZ. |
| Placing Orders — set 2 | We would like to place the following order, 7 units of AP34 and 10 units of AP24.; Would you please send us 7 units of AP34.; According to your website AP34 is not in stock until 7 February 2013, is that correct? |
| Asking for further information | I would like to know if my order can be changed.; Could you send me the product specifications?; I have a few questions about your company´s Terms & Conditions. |
| Replies — set 1 | Thank you for your email of 13 November 2013 enquiring about Product Series 2TZ.; Thank you for your email of 7 March 2013 asking for information about Product Series 2TZ.; With reference to your email of 9 April 2013, I am sending you the updated product specifications. |
| Replies — set 2 | We would like to thank you for your email of 5 March 2013 enquiring about Product Series 2TZ.; We would like to thank you for your email of 5 March 2012 asking for information about Product Series 2TZ. |
| Replies — set 3 | I have attached our product catalogue to this email.; We will dispatch the products within 24 hours of receiving your order payment confirmation.; You will note that our products are delivered within 48 hours of receiving payment. |
| Closing Sentences — set 1 | If you require more information please don't hesitate to contact me.; Once again I apologize for any inconvenience.; I look forward to your reply. |
| Closing Sentences — set 2 | I look forward to hearing from you shortly.; If you need any further information, please don’t hesitate to contact me.; If you have any further questions, please contact me. |
| End | Best regards, / Regards, / Kind regards, |
